Purpose

DVRPC maintains a comprehensive bicycle network dataset for the 9-county region but has no formal process for collecting updates as new bicycle facilities are installed.

This map is a proof-of-concept for a simple interface that allows DVRPC staff and other planning partners to provide updates to the regional bicycle network dataset.

The base bicycle network is shown with thin colored lines, and modifications provided by users are shown with a black underline and a thicker, dashed, colored line.

Directions

  • Select the bicycle facility type using the drop-down form below
  • Click on the box icon at the top-right corner of the map to begin drawing a polygon
  • Click as many times as needed to add vertex points to your polygon
  • Double-click to end your polygon
  • ... the map will then send your design and selected links to the API, and the map will refresh showing your edit with a black outline ...
